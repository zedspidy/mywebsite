Story part 2

When Ajay reaches home, his mother is shocked and asks why he came on leave. Then Ajay tells her everything. Hearing this, his parents become nervous because they never wanted this truth to come before him. But suddenly his mother starts crying. Ajay: What happened, Mom? Why are you crying? Is there something you are hiding from me? Mother: Son, there is a very big truth that I must tell you today. You are not my only child — you had a twin brother too. From childhood you were intelligent, but he was weak. Because of this, everyone at school praised you. But he could not bear it. At home, relatives and neighbors all admired you. Out of jealousy, he stayed locked in his room. Mother thought that maybe with time his feelings of jealousy would fade… But we never knew that by staying locked in his room he had started practicing black magic. When you both were 8 years old, your health began to deteriorate. We consulted many doctors but no one understood the cause. Then one night I heard some strange sounds from your brother’s room. When I went there, I was terrified to see him sitting inside a circle with a star drawn in it. I quickly called your father. When your father saw this, he immediately extinguished the ritual fire and slapped your brother hard on the cheek. But then a strong wind began to blow, and we both became frightened. Suddenly your brother started floating in the air… It was as if another spirit had taken control of his body. He spoke in a different voice, saying that he was leaving now but would surely return after 10 years — and then he would take his revenge. After this, his hands twisted unnaturally, his veins burst, and he died right there. But the terrifying part is that now 10 years have already passed, and you have started hearing voices — at exactly 12:50 AM, the same time your brother died. Hearing all this, Ajay becomes very scared. He withdraws his name from school and stays at home. But even then, strange incidents keep happening to him — like feeling someone’s presence, hearing whispers, and sometimes even finding his belongings moved to different places
